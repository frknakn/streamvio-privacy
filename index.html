Privacy Policy — Last Updated: March 17, 2026

Introduction

StreamVio ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our StreamVio mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Playlist Data: URLs, usernames, and passwords for IPTV services that you add to the app. This data is stored locally on your device only.
  • Subscription Information: When you purchase a subscription, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience.
  • Device Information: Basic device information for troubleshooting and compatibility purposes.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal data
  • We do not share any data with third parties for advertising
  • We do not store your IPTV credentials on our servers

Data Storage

All your data (playlists, favorites, watch history, preferences) is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure data storage framework. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

Third-Party Services

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat for subscription management. For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Apple App Store

Subscriptions and payments are processed through Apple's App Store, governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Security

All data stored on your device is protected by your device's built-in security features, including encryption.

Your Rights

  • Delete your data at any time by removing playlists or uninstalling the app
  • Manage subscriptions through your Apple ID settings
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
